## Defensive Structure

The portfolio is designed to provide layered protection:

1. **Discovery Layer (Patent A):** Protects the physics cliff finding, making it impossible to implement azimuthal stiffness control without licensing
2. **Method Layer (Patent B):** Protects the specific ILC control approach that is the only validated solution
3. **System Layer (Patent C):** Protects the manufacturing pipeline and provenance chain
4. **Extension Layer (Patents D, E):** Extends protection to adjacent domains and form factors
